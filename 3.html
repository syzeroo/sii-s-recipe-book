How to make "Devonshire Honey Cake"


Ingredients
cake or all purpose flour 200g
baking powder 5g
salt 1g
honey 160g
unsalted butter 150g
sugar/muscovado 50g
2 eggs


Step1 Melt the butter, sugar and Honey
Step2 Grease the prepared pan
Step3 Shift dry Ingredients
Step4 Add the eggs to the honey mixtrue
Step5 Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture
Step6 Pour the batter into the prepared pan
Step7 Preheat oven to 320 degree F and bake for 40minutes
